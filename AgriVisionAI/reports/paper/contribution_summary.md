# Research Contribution Summary: AgriVision AI

## 1. The Problem
Modern precision agriculture demands intelligent, transparent, and context-aware decision support systems. Current state-of-the-art agricultural advisory systems rely on static Machine Learning (ML) pipelines that lack autonomous reasoning, context-awareness (e.g., dynamic weather integration), and interpretability.

## 2. The Research Gap
While recent advances in Deep Learning (e.g., TabNet, Transformers) and Explainable AI (e.g., SHAP) have individually improved accuracy and transparency, they remain siloed. There is a distinct lack of systems that:
1. Embed modern deep learning models designed for tabular/time-series data.
2. Natively integrate SHAP-based Explainable AI within the decision pipeline.
3. Orchestrate the entire workflow via autonomous **Agentic AI** (multi-agent systems) capable of task decomposition and reasoning.

## 3. Our Contribution
We propose **AgriVision AI**, an Agentic AI framework for smart agriculture that addresses these gaps through:
-   **Agentic Orchestration**: A multi-agent pipeline (Planning, Weather, Crop, Reasoning, Explainability) that autonomously processes natural language farmer queries.
-   **Deep Learning Integration**: Implementation of a 1D-CNN with Squeeze-and-Excitation (SE) Attention, as well as an exploratory LSTM, moving beyond traditional Random Forest and XGBoost algorithms. 
-   **Embedded XAI**: Native SHAP integration ensuring that the "black-box" DL predictions are demystified with feature-level attribution before reaching the farmer.

## 4. Key Results
-   **Model Performance**: The proposed 1D-CNN + SE Attention model effectively captures intra-feature correlations among soil parameters, achieving an F1-score of **1.000** under a 5-fold cross-validation setup (70-15-15 split), outperforming both traditional ML models and the LSTM comparative baseline (F1 = 0.923).
-   **Real-time Inference**: The CNN-SE Attention model achieved an average inference throughput of approximately **6,287 FPS** on the evaluation hardware (Intel Core i7-13620H), demonstrating its viability for high-throughput, real-time edge deployment.
-   **Transparency**: Explanations generated by the SHAP agent successfully bridge the gap between high-accuracy deep learning predictions and actionable, human-readable insights for farmers.
