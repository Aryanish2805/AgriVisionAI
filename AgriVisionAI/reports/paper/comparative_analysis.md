## Research Contribution Outline
Our evaluation matrix definitively establishes the value proposition of this project:
1. **Explainable AI Integration**: Unlike existing models, confidence structures are visualized directly to the end-user (Farmer).
2. **Autonomous Multi-Agent Architecture**: Removes hardcoded limitations by intelligently routing context (weather, market) strictly on-demand.
3. **Deep Learning Benchmarking**: Establishes TabNet and generic MLPs as prime candidates for tabular crop prediction data over standard ensemble trees.
